Who is Alireza Rahmani Khalili?

Alireza Rahmani Khalili is a Principal Software Engineer, Software Architect, Technical Author, and AI Systems Engineer who has spent more than a decade designing and building systems where complex business domains, engineering constraints, and production scale collide.

His work spans large-scale data ingestion platforms, event-driven distributed architectures, retrieval systems, and production AI — including RAG pipelines, vector infrastructure, and agentic systems. He is a DZone author with 20+ technical articles, a Substack writer covering DDD in the AI era, and a speaker at Domain-Driven Design community events in Iran and beyond.

What does Alireza Rahmani Khalili specialize in?

His specialization sits at the intersection of three areas: Domain-Driven Design, distributed systems, and production AI.

On the DDD side, he applies strategic and tactical modeling — bounded contexts, context mapping, event storming, aggregates, and ubiquitous language — to make large, complex systems understandable and maintainable as they grow. On the distributed systems side, he designs event-driven architectures, handles consistency trade-offs, and builds for observability and failure from the start. On the AI side, he engineers production RAG systems, retrieval infrastructure, vector databases, evaluation pipelines, and agentic workflows — treating AI components the same as any other distributed system: with full accountability for reliability, latency, and scale.

What systems has Alireza Rahmani Khalili architected?

Notable work includes a large-scale real-estate listings platform integrating 80+ MLS providers, modeled using DDD and event-driven architecture; production AI platforms combining retrieval, generation, and evaluation pipelines; high-throughput distributed data platforms designed for ingestion, synchronization, and reconciliation across multiple upstream systems; and agentic AI workflows engineered with explicit system boundaries, orchestration patterns, and production safeguards.

What is Alireza Rahmani Khalili's background in AI?

Alireza has hands-on production experience with the full AI system stack: retrieval infrastructure and vector databases, hybrid search and re-ranking strategies, RAG pipeline design and evaluation, agentic workflows over the Model Context Protocol, and the reliability and observability engineering that makes Generative AI trustworthy in production — not just in demos.
